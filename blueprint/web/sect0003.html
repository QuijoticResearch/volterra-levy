3.2 The Two-Parameter VRNC

Definition 32 Girsanov data

GirsanovData Continuous and jump market prices of risk \(\theta ^W\), \(\theta ^J\), diffusion coefficient \(\sigma _L\), and jump drift \(\Phi ^J\).

Definition 33 Effective drift rate

CombinedEnergySpace.effectiveDriftRate def:GirsanovData \(\Psi := -\sigma _L \theta ^W + \Phi ^J\). Defined, not assumed.

Definition 34 VRNC data

VRNCData def:GirsanovData, def:VolterraKernel Extends Girsanov data with kernel, drift rate \(\mu \), risk-free rate \(r\), Lévy exposure \(\lambda \), memory loading \(\beta \), and drift functional \(B_t^\theta = \int _0^t K(t,s)\Psi (s)\, ds\).

Theorem 35 \(\Psi \) formula

CombinedEnergySpace.VRNC_determines_Psi def:effectiveDriftRate \(\Psi _t = -\sigma _L \theta ^W_t + \Phi ^J_t\). ALGEBRAIC: unfolds the definition.

Proof â–¼

rfl.

Theorem 36 VRNC continuous recovery

CombinedEnergySpace.VRNC_continuous_recovery def:effectiveDriftRate When \(\nu = 0\): \(\Psi = -\sigma _L \theta ^W\). ALGEBRAIC: substitutes \(\Phi ^J = 0\).

Proof â–¼

simp [effectiveDriftRate, hno_jumps].

Theorem 37 \(\theta ^W\) from \(\Psi \) and \(\Phi ^J\)

CombinedEnergySpace.continuous_from_jump_choice def:effectiveDriftRate \(\theta ^W_t = (\Phi ^J_t - \Psi ) / \sigma _L\). ALGEBRAIC: pure algebra (field_simp + linarith).

Proof â–¼

Unfold, field_simp, linarith.
